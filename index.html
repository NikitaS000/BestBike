Trek Bicycle Corporation is one of the largest bicycle manufacturers. Trek bicycles are sold in North America, Europe and Asia. In 2010, Trek teamed up with several suppliers to provide environmentally friendly products. These products include new bicycles that are made of steel. Steel is a very simple alloy to recycle.

Giant Bicycles Co. Ltd. is one of the largest manufacturers of branded bicycles in the world. At the moment, The Global Bicycle Company owns the largest production facilities (seven factories in China, one in Taiwan, one in the Netherlands and one in Hungary). Today, about 55% of all branded bicycles are manufactured at Giant factories

Specialized Bicycle Components, better known as Specialized, is an American manufacturer of bicycles and equipment for cycling. It is the fourth largest bicycle company in the United States and a leading innovator in the bicycle industry. Founded by Mike Sinyard in 1974. The headquarters is located in Morgan Hill, California. The company produces equipment for a number of cycling disciplines: mountain biking, road cycling, BMX, cyclocross, as well as hybrid, road, children's bicycles and electric bicycles.

Scott Sports SA (formerly Scott USA) is a Swiss producer of bicycles, winter equipment, motorsports gear and sportswear. The company's main office is in Givisiez, Switzerland, with branches in Europe, the United States, South Africa and India.

Cannondale Bicycle Corporation is an American division of the Dutch company Pon Holdings, which is engaged in the production of bicycles. The main office is located in Wilton, Connecticut with manufacturing and assembly in China, the Netherlands and Taiwan.
