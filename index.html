  • Welcome to our bicycle information site!

  • We have sections dedicated to various types of bicycles, such as road, mountain, urban and BMX. We offer reviews of popular models, tips on choosing a bike and accessories, as well as detailed instructions on how to care for your two-wheeled friend.
  • Be inspired by our articles about the benefits of cycling for health and the environment. We talk about the best routes for skiing and organizing bicycle trips. We also have tips on road safety and rules of conduct for all cyclists.

Trek Bicycle Corporation is one of the largest bicycle manufacturers. Trek bicycles are sold in North America, Europe and Asia. In 2010, Trek teamed up with several suppliers to provide environmentally friendly products. These products include new bicycles that are made of steel. Steel is a very simple alloy to recycle.

Giant Bicycles Co. Ltd. is one of the largest manufacturers of branded bicycles in the world. At the moment, The Global Bicycle Company owns the largest production facilities (seven factories in China, one in Taiwan, one in the Netherlands and one in Hungary). Today, about 55% of all branded bicycles are manufactured at Giant factories

Specialized Bicycle Components, better known as Specialized, is an American manufacturer of bicycles and equipment for cycling. It is the fourth largest bicycle company in the United States and a leading innovator in the bicycle industry. Founded by Mike Sinyard in 1974. The headquarters is located in Morgan Hill, California. The company produces equipment for a number of cycling disciplines: mountain biking, road cycling, BMX, cyclocross, as well as hybrid, road, children's bicycles and electric bicycles.

Scott Sports SA (formerly Scott USA) is a Swiss producer of bicycles, winter equipment, motorsports gear and sportswear. The company's main office is in Givisiez, Switzerland, with branches in Europe, the United States, South Africa and India.

Cannondale Bicycle Corporation is an American division of the Dutch company Pon Holdings, which is engaged in the production of bicycles. The main office is located in Wilton, Connecticut with manufacturing and assembly in China, the Netherlands and Taiwan.

Marlin 7 Gen 2

Marlin 7 is where race-worthy mountain bikes begin. The smooth, lightweight RockShox fork and upgraded parts make this model the best choice for new riders who want a fast cross country hardtail that will hold its own against our higher-end race bikes.

Propel Advanced PRO

BikeRadar called the new Propel Advanced Pro "a brilliant all-rounder aero road bike that offers a fast, fun ride at a competitive price." The specific model tested was the Propel Advanced Pro 0 AXS, which pairs a lightweight composite frameset featuring AeroSystem Shaping with a Giant SLR 1 50 Carbon WheelSystem and SRAM Force eTap AXS wireless electronic drivetrain.

Scott Addict RC Ultimate (2020)

The new SCOTT Addict RC is the benchmark for the perfect road bike when it comes to professional racing. It is designed taking into account all the requirements of the Mitchelton-SCOTT World Tour team, as well as the ambitions of amateurs with a professional bias. Extremely lightweight, with the best performance-focused composite frame structure, plus it's a bike with a whole new philosophy of integration and aerodynamics.

Cannondale SCALPEL HT HM

Scalpel HT is one of the most aggressive, progressive and innovative cross-country hardtails ever created. Extremely easy to climb and explosive in acceleration, it will satisfy even the most picky racers.
